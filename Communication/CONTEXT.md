## Communication Notes

_Key decisions, preferences, or constraints communicated informally by the client (calls, messages, emails) that haven't been captured elsewhere._

- 2026-03-31: Initial brief received. Prototype scope is design-only (no backend). Focus on stakeholder review and developer handoff.
- 2026-03-31: Client shared Happy Visitor user manuals (Employee + Security) and a stakeholder pain points document. Key pain points from stakeholders:
  - **Front Desk:** Needs instant dashboard visibility — who's expected, who's on premises, pending actions. Currently lacks advance knowledge of daily visitors, can't monitor who's present, suffers from extended wait times and redundant manual steps.
  - **Employees:** Circumvent the system because the visit request process is too cumbersome. Need quick raise/accept/reject on mobile, status transparency, flexible cancellation. High no-show rates cause downstream delays.
  - **Management:** Visitor data exists but is inaccessible for analysis. Need cross-location trends, repeat customer flagging, and lead generation from visitor data.
  - **Strategic:** Priorities are internal data ownership, operational workflow alignment, and converting visitor intelligence into business development opportunities.
